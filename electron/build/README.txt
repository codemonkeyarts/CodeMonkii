App icon assets — used by the desktop window, taskbar, splash screen, and
NSIS installer.

  icon-source.png   master artwork (MONKII monkey with cyan glasses, 1080px)
  icon.ico          multi-size Windows icon (16/24/32/48/64/128/256)
  icon.png          256px render

To regenerate after changing the artwork: resize icon-source.png to the sizes
above (e.g. with sharp) and assemble them with png-to-ico.
